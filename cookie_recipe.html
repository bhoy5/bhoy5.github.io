RELEASE OF MY GRANDMA’S COOKIE RECIPE!

Here is my grandma's recipe for her famous chocolate chip cookies!

My grandma’s recipe is well known and many people are excited to make them!

My Grandma’s Perfected Ingredients

1. 6 cups flower
2. 2 cup brown sugar
3. 2 cup granulated sugar
4. 4 eggs
5. 1 tbs baking soda
6. 1 tsp salt
7. 2 tsp vanilla
8. 2 Cups choc chips
